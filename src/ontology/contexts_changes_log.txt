## Assignment Strategy

The script used a hierarchical pattern-matching approach based on:

1. **Label content analysis**: Keywords indicating specific contexts
   - Ecosystem-level: "ecosystem", "biome"
   - Irrigation types: "surface irrigation", "subsurface irrigation"
   - Fertilization: "band", "non-band", "fertilizer"
   - Weather/climate: "precipitation", "rainfall", "snowpack"
   - Fire: "fire"
   - Growth: "growth stage", "phenological"

2. **Spatial contexts**:
   - Surface vs subsurface
   - Canopy, root, branch specific locations
   - Boundary layer

3. **Process contexts**:
   - Photosynthesis pathways (C4 carbon fixation)
   - Infection/nodulation
   - Runoff, precipitation

4. **Measured_in inference**: When label doesn't explicitly state context, inferred from measured_in column:
   - Soil processes (mineralization, decomposition)
   - Plant processes (photosynthesis, transpiration)
   - Root processes (uptake, fixation)
   - Canopy processes
