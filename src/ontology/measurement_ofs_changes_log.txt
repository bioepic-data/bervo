## Assignment Strategy

The script used a hierarchical pattern-matching approach:

1. **Excluded non-applicable entries**: Equilibrium constants, rate constants, indices, flags, etc. → NA
2. **Ratios**: Identified and assigned specific ratio terms (e.g., "Nitrogen to carbon ratio")
3. **Compounds first**: CO₂, CH₄, N₂O, NH₄, etc. (most specific)
4. **Organic compounds**: DOC, DON, DOP, soil organic matter, etc. (to avoid element over-matching)
5. **Elements**: C, N, P, O, etc. (after compounds to prevent false matches)
6. **Biological materials**: Leaf, root, branch, soil, plant, microbes, etc.
7. **Physical substances**: Water, air, heat, ice, salt, etc.
8. **Attribute-based inference**: Respiration → Carbon, Temperature → Heat
9. **Pattern extraction**: "of X" patterns for flux/concentration variables
